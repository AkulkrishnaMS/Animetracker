## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change the order?**  
A: Currently, anime are ranked in the order you add them (1-10). Future updates will add drag-and-drop reordering!

**Q: Can I add manga to Top 10?**  
A: Currently only anime can be added to Top 10. This may change in future updates!

**Q: Where is my Top 10 saved?**  
A: It's saved in your browser's localStorage, so it persists across sessions.

**Q: Can others see my Top 10?**  
A: Yes! It's displayed prominently on your profile page for everyone to see.

**Q: What's the difference between Top 10 and Favorites?**  
A: 
- **Top 10**: Your absolute favorites, ranked 1-10, displayed with golden style
- **Favorites**: Unlimited anime/manga you like, displayed in a grid
